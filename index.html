Speakers are core contributors and industry thought leaders working at companies that push for innovation

SANNA MARIN - Former Prime Minister & Head of Government of Finland (2019-2023)

Sanna Marin is the Former Prime Minister of Finland (2019-2023) and the leader of the Finnish Social Democratic Party. Appointed at the age of 34, Marin served as the youngest Prime Minister of the world when taking office in 2019.

DAVID PLOUFFE - Obama Campaign Manager & Senior Advisor

A pioneering force of innovation in politics, technology, and grassroots leadership, David Plouffe has distinguished himself as a proven field general and idea generator. He served as Campaign Manager of Barack Obama's historic victory in 2008

KENDRA SCOTT - Self-Made Billionaire & Philanthropist

Founder, chief creative officer, best-selling author, and philanthropist, Kendra Scott, started her company in 2002, just three months after her first son was born. With only $500 and a tea box to carry her collection, she went store to store selling her designs.

DE KAI - Statesman of AI, AI Professor at HKUST

De Kai is a pioneering AI scientist who is widely regarded as “the Statesman of AI.” A founder of the contemporary AI field as known today, three decades ago De Kai invented and built the world’s first global-scale online language translator.

LAURIE SEGALL - Former Senior Tech Correspondent

Silicon Valley technology reporter Laurie Segall is the go-to source for businesses, investors, and decision-makers seeking to understand and leverage the power of technology.

KIZZMEKIA CORBETT - Lead Scientist in COVID-19

Dr. Kizzmekia Corbett-Helaire was the scientific lead of the Vaccine Research Center’s coronavirus team at the U.S. National Institutes of Health where she studied coronavirus biology and vaccine development.

SIR TIM BERNERS-LEE - Inventor of the World Wide Web

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the most powerful communication medium in the world — the World Wide Web — and gave it to the world for free. It is no exaggeration to say that Sir Tim’s visionary work has transformed every aspect of our lives.
